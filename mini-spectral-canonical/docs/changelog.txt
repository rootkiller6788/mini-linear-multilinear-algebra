MiniSpectralCanonical Changelog
================================

v0.1.0 (2026-05-21)
  - Initial standalone mini-package with full skeleton
  - Core: Spectral Theorem, Jordan/Rational forms, SVD, Polar, spectral radius
  - Morphisms: Similarity, UnitaryEquivalence, Congruence, SpectralIso, SVDIso
  - Constructions: BlockDiagonal, Invariant/Eigen/GeneralizedEigen subspaces
  - Properties: Trace, det, char/min poly, multiplicities, JordanType, Signature
  - Theorems: Spectral theorems, Primary decomposition, Classifications, Schur
  - Examples: Identity, Diagonal, Jordan, Nilpotent, Counterexamples
  - Bridges: ToAlgebra (F[t]-module), ToTopology (spectral radius), ToGeometry (SVD), ToComputation (QR)
  - Tests: Smoke, Examples, Regression
  - Benchmarks: CoreCoverage, Princeton, Cambridge, Harvard, MIT, Oxford
